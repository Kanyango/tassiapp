Our History

Tassia Catholic Primary & Nursery School is a learning complex comprising of a Pre-school and Primary School. The school is located in Tassia Estate of Nairobi's Embakasi area. The school belongs to the Catholic arch-diocese of Nairobi. The Catholic school is all about excellence and inculcating in the pupils the virtues that will make them complete wholesome human beings mentally, physically and spiritually. Since its inception in 2005 by Rev. Fr. Robert Vujs and under the guidance of the Congregation of Carmelite Religious (CCR) sisters and the Archdiocese of Nairobi , the school has lept from strength to strength even in its nascent stage. In the K.C.P.E examination of 2011, only the second for the school to sit, the pupils posted exemplary results making the school rank 25th in the whole of Nairobi County and sending at least 10 of our candidates to top-performing national schools among them Starehe Boys' Centre, Moi Girls' Eldoret, Strathmore school, Moi Forces Academy Nairobi, Mang'u High School and Maranda High School.


About Us

We are a dedicated Catholic school, striving ever hard to improve the standards of our pupils in all aspects of life. Being an acdemic institution, our first mandate is to ensure the propagation of knowledge to the young ones, a task we perform relentlessly and to the best of our abilities through the enabling hands of the Almighty God. However, in living true to our vision and mission, we go beyond the normal to ensure the nurturing of the pupils into wholesome citizens. We expose and encourage our pupils to participate in competitive sports, arts and music as well as inculcating in them the fear of the Lord and bringing thyem up with a conscious religious responsibility.
